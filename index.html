MINERIDLE


A Javascript incremental game by @fentonizer

Welcome to Miner-Idle! Peridot is already being mined, but we can only find the common variety for now. Sell these and purchase upgrades to increase what you can find and the likelihood of finding it. Your total cash and helpful tips are displayed at the bottom of the screen.

The number next to the minerals' name is how often (in milliseconds) your team will go mining. They won't always find something! Increase this with the Odds+ button and increase the speed of mining with the Tick+ button.
